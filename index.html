Good to know

Questions, answered

What happens if you don't reach 35 orders?

If the run doesn't fill, your payment is refunded in full — no risk to you. You're reserving a spot, not gambling on it.

Why preorder instead of just buying one?

A custom run like this only makes sense at volume. Preorders are what make the per-watch cost work and prove the demand needed to start production. It's how a watch this specific gets made in the first place.

Is $350 really a fair price?

Yes. You're getting a genuine automatic (not quartz) dive watch with a custom dial, exhibition case back, a metal bracelet and a genuine-leather band. Comparable automatics with this level of finishing routinely run well past $350 — and none of them carry the squadron's dolphins.

When will I get my watch?

Production begins as soon as the run is filled. You'll receive progress updates from order to delivery so you're never in the dark on timing.

How do I pick my options?

You choose your finish (Gold-Tone or Steel/Gunmetal-Tone), case size (42.5mm men's or 38mm women's), and dolphins (Gold for officers or Silver for enlisted) right on the order form.

Is this official / who makes it?

It's a custom community watch produced in partnership with Tenacity Watches. It's made by and for the COMSUBRON ONE community.
